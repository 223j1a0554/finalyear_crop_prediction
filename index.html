🌾 Crop Prediction System Using Satellite and Weather Data

Why This Project?

Agriculture is highly dependent on environmental conditions such as rainfall, temperature, humidity, and water availability. Due to climate change and unpredictable weather patterns, it has become difficult for farmers to make accurate decisions about crop selection.

Problem Statement

  1. Farmers lack access to accurate and real-time prediction systems.

  2. Weather conditions are unpredictable and constantly changing.

  3. Traditional crop selection methods are not data-driven.

  4. Water availability is often ignored or misjudged.

  5. Wrong crop selection leads to low yield and financial loss.

Our Solution

This project provides an intelligent Crop Prediction System using Deep Learning that helps users make better agricultural decisions.

  1. Uses Deep Neural Networks for accurate prediction.

  2. Integrates real-time weather data (temperature, humidity, rainfall)

  3. Detects nearby water sources using location coordinates.

  4. Predicts crop growth suitability (High / Medium / Low)

  5. Suggests better crops when the selected crop is not suitable.

  6. Displays results using graphs and map visualization.
